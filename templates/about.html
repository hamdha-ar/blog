About Me

This is what I do.

Hello and welcome! I'm an Associate Software Engineer at Gramener, a Straive company. I graduated from NIT Calicut with a degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), but my real passion has always been coding and software development.

Born and raised in Kerala, I was always fascinated by technology—whether it was breaking apart gadgets, debugging code, or experimenting with new programming languages. My journey into development started during my college years, where I explored AI, machine learning, and full-stack development.

Now, I specialize in building scalable applications, AI-driven solutions, and web technologies. This blog is my creative space where I share insights, tech experiences, and coding challenges. If you love talking about AI, software engineering, or emerging tech, let’s connect and grow together!
